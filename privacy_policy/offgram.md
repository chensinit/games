# Privacy Policy – Offgram

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Offgram (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.
This application is designed to function without collecting personal data from users.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Offgram mobile application.

---

## 1. No Account Registration

Offgram does **not require account creation**.
You can use the app without signing up, logging in, or providing any personal identification information.

We do not collect:

* Email address
* Phone number
* Address
* Government identification

---

## 2. Local Storage Only

All content created inside the app, including:

* Posts
* Personal notes
* Comments
* Profile settings

is stored **only on your device**.

The data remains locally on your phone and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do not operate user data servers for Offgram.

---

## 3. No Data Collection

Offgram does **not collect or store personal information** from users.

We do not:

* Track user activity across apps
* Collect personal identifiers
* Access contacts, photos, or device files
* Upload your written content

---

## 4. No Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect user data, we do not sell, share, or transfer personal information to third parties.

---

## 5. Analytics and Advertising

Offgram does **not use advertising networks** and does not use third-party analytics services to track user behavior.

---

## 6. Permissions

The app only requests permissions necessary for basic functionality (such as local storage access, if applicable).
No sensitive permissions are required.

---

## 7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

* Deleting a post removes it immediately from the device.
* Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

We cannot recover deleted data because we do not keep copies.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Offgram does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Because the app does not collect data, children can use the app without providing personal information.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app functionality changes.
The updated version will always be available at the same URL with a revised date.

---
